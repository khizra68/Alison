About Us

Allison Barclay joined the Governing Board in 2021. Allison and her husband and four children moved to Temecula in 2013. She currently has one daughter attending Temecula Valley High School, along with two older daughters who graduated from Temecula Valley and Chaparral High Schools, and a son who graduated from Fallbrook High School.
